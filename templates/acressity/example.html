Below is a picture of his journey page on this site.

He's currently featuring his experience {{ bugsy.featured_experience }}, meaning he is focusing on achieving this experience at the present moment. If {{ bugsy.first_name }} wishes to keep any experience private, he has the ability of doing so.

Currently, he can develop these experiences by writing narratives (basically blog posts/updates), uploading images, inviting other explorers, creating a bried (short bio), and establishing means of accepting donations (PayPal). This list will expand in the future to include greater coverage of how experiences are realized, recorded, preserved, and evolve—the simulacrum of consciousness.

Now let's talk about you. Like everyone else on this planet, you have high aspirations. This aspiring pulls you towards greatness like a muffled voice in the distance encouraging you to explore and experience life, a yearning for truth. Yet these calls from the distance are too easily forgotten or surrendered for any number of reasons. This app aims to keep this tarnishing from becoming inhibition. It encourages you to pursue your dreams, to have you be set ablaze for the rest of the world to behold, to rage against the dying of the light, to rise and stand up for your beliefs.

Compose a list of experiences you wish to have and notice how simply writing them down makes them somehow more real. Draw from this newly created power to charge yourself to following through on realizing these desires of the heart. Use this app to hoist yourself beyond your comfort zone into the realm of growth and thriving.
